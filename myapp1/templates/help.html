To-do List

This web app is built for dedicated people like you to make their day productive . Dayone app help you to make todo list regularly and if you finishe the task you can view the completed task on clicking the completed tasks button , and you can shift tasks from completed tasks list to tasks list you are not satisfied about the task you have accomplished, on clicking move to tasks button .

Journal

You can also write daily journal ,so that you can log you experiences every day . on clicking on text area under the 'write your journal entry' you will be able to enter your experience and if you wish you can even edit the journal on clicking the edit button . you can also view your previous entries on clicking view all link just next to edit button.
